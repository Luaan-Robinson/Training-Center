Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior experience to enroll?

Most of our programs are designed for beginners with no prior experience. However, some advanced programs like AI & Data Science and Cloud & DevOps may require basic programming knowledge. During your consultation, we'll assess your skills and recommend the right starting point.

What are the payment options?

We offer flexible payment plans including monthly installments, upfront payment discounts (save up to 15%), employer sponsorship programs, and SETA funding assistance. Payment plans can be spread over the duration of your program with no interest charges.

What is your job placement rate?

85% of our graduates secure employment within 6 months of completing their training. We have partnerships with over 20 companies actively hiring our graduates, and our career services team provides ongoing support until you land your ideal role.

Are your certifications recognized?

Yes! All our programs are SAQA-accredited, and we prepare students for industry-recognized certifications from CompTIA, Microsoft, Cisco, AWS, and Google. These certifications are globally recognized and highly valued by employers.

Can I study part-time while working?

Absolutely! We offer evening classes (6pm-9pm) and weekend programs specifically designed for working professionals. Many of our successful graduates completed their training while maintaining full-time employment.
